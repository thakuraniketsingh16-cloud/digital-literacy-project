# Task 4 Part B: Social Media Do's and Don'ts

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts. | Don't post sensitive personal info like your home address. |
| Think before you post; assume everything is permanent. | Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or online hate. |
| Verify information before sharing to avoid fake news. | Don't use unprofessional language that could hurt your career. |
| Credit original creators when sharing their content. | Don't share your login credentials or OTPs with anyone. |
| Use professional photos for academic/career platforms. | Don't tag others in embarrassing photos without permission. |
